ABSTRACT

 In today’s rapidly modernizing world, the traditional knowledge and practice of healthy lifestyle habits and natural healing for common illnesses are gradually declining. Many rural communities face limited access to medical facilities due to factors such as distance, cost, and lack of awareness. This highlights the growing need to revive and promote traditional health care methods as an accessible and sustainable alternative. To address this challenge, our community service project titled "Traditional Health Care Methods" was initiated with the goal of raising awareness about healthy living practices. The project focused on promoting traditional remedies for common ailments and encouraging the use of Ayurvedic medicine as a natural and effective solution to health concerns. The initiative specifically aimed at educating homemakers—who play a crucial role in family health management—on simple and practical herbal remedies. It also engaged school children to foster early adoption of yoga and mindfulness practices, ensuring the transmission of culturally rooted health knowledge to future generations. Additionally, elderly individuals were guided on the benefits of Ayurvedic treatments, reinforcing the importance of traditional medicine in supporting holistic well-being.
