CONCLUSION

 Our project, “Traditional Health Care Methods,” was rooted in the goal of addressing rural community health challenges by reviving age-old, natural practices. Throughout the project, we reached out to different groups with targeted wellness solutions:

  • Elderly individuals were supported with simple, safe Ayurvedic remedies to manage their common health concerns.
  • Homemakers were empowered with practical home remedies using easily available kitchen ingredients for everyday ailments.
  • School children were engaged through regular yoga and meditation sessions, helping them develop focus, discipline, and emotional balance.

 This project not only served the community but also helped us build awareness, empathy, and a deeper understanding of holistic health practices. As we conclude, we hope the knowledge and care shared will continue to benefit these communities and inspire a return to nature-based well-being.
